About

Life is like a spinning wheel.

Aidan is currently pursuing a PhD in Environmental Science and Engineering from Harvard University under Professor Kaighin McColl. Aidan's research interests are in ecohydrology and land-plant-atmosphere interactions. He graduated Summa Cum Laude from Princeton University with a Bachelors in Civil and Environmental Engineering. In his spare time he enjoys writing about himself in the third person and designing amateurish (is that a word?) websites.
